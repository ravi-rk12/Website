About

I am a qualified and experienced tutor offering online classes in Maths and Physics for students in classes 6-10. I focus on providing a comprehensive understanding of the subjects and tailor my teaching to the needs and learning style of each individual student. With my classes, students can choose the topic or chapter they want to learn and can opt for chapter-wise payment to build trust.

Classes

I offer online classes on the 2nd and 4th Saturday of every month, and every Sunday. The timings can be determined based on the student's preferences, as well as the formation of similar groups based on class and topic. I can also teach face-to-face if the student prefers.

Pricing

My classes can be paid for chapter-wise, so students can try a class and see if they find it useful before committing to more. The cost will be determined based on the chapter and number of hours required for teaching. Please contact me for more details.
